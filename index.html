About

Biography

Dr. Jonathan A. Carter is a Full Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, University of Technology. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from MIT in 2012.

His research focuses on developing machine learning systems that are interpretable, fair, and robust—particularly in high-stakes domains such as healthcare and autonomous systems. He leads the Trustworthy AI Lab and has collaborated with industry partners including Google Research and DeepMind.

He has received multiple best paper awards and serves on the editorial boards of NeurIPS, ICML, and the Journal of Machine Learning Research.
