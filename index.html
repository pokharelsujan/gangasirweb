About

Biography

An Associate Professor of Mathematics at Kathmandu University, Dhulikhel, Nepal. PhD in Mathematics was obtained from the Department of Mathematics at Kathmandu University in 2018 under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Dil Bahadur Gurung.

MPhil in Mathematics was completed from Kathmandu University in 2013, and a Master's degree in Mathematics was obtained from Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu, Nepal, in 2006.

Research interests include mathematical modeling, particularly in infectious disease dynamics and prey–predator systems, along with numerical methods for ordinary and partial differential equations (ODEs and PDEs) and computational mathematics.
